# Hinge — Platform Mechanics

> Pulled from `APPS/PLATFORMS.md`. The hard limits and structural facts for Hinge profiles.

## Profile Structure

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Photos/Videos | **6 slots** (minimum 4 required) |
| Prompts | **3 displayed** (150 chars each) |
| Bio | No separate bio — **prompts ARE the bio** |
| Total prompts available | **~105** |

## Prompt Types

- **Text prompts** — standard written answers
- **Photo prompts** — pair a prompt with a photo
- **Video prompts** — record a video answer
- **Voice prompts** — audio response (30s)
- **Prompt Polls** — matches choose which answer to respond to

## Key Stats

- Likes on **text prompts are 47% more likely** to lead to dates than likes on photos
- Filling all 3 prompt slots = **73% more quality matches**
- **Voice prompts → 32% more dates** (under 5% of users use them — massive differentiator)
- **Most Compatible** feature = **8x date likelihood** vs. ordinary likes
- Recommended strategy: 1 humor + 1 depth + 1 conversation-starter

## Algorithm

- **Gale-Shapley mutual fit** matching (game-theory pairing algorithm, not just engagement-driven feed)
- Inputs: **commented likes** (highest signal), response time, profile completeness
- Verification matters; complete profile to 100% before going live
- Peak hours: **Sun–Wed 7–10 PM**; weekday morning 7–9 AM has lowest competition

## Unique Features

- "Designed to be deleted" — relationship-focused positioning
- Likes/comments on specific photos or prompts (not whole-profile swipe)
- Roses (premium like)
- Standouts feed
- No swiping — intentional profile review
