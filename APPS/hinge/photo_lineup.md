# Hinge — Recommended 6-Photo Lineup

> Output of Pass 5 (per-platform analysis) applied to your curated inventory. See full pass at `../photo-ranker/results/PASS_5_per_platform.md`.

**Audience:** 25–40, slightly more relationship-minded than Tinder, women feel safer here than other swipe apps.
**What works:** Photos that are *comment-able* — anything that invites a question. Photos that pair well with prompts. Authenticity > polish.
**What loses:** Generic glamour. Polished-without-personality.
