# Tinder — Bio + Prompts

A complete Tinder profile package: **one tight bio** (Tinder's 15–45 word optimal) + **3 prompts × 10 answer options each** (30 prompt answers in total).

All bios and prompts respect Tinder's hard limits:
- Bio: ≤500 chars, optimal 15–45 words
- Each prompt: ≤124 chars

## Why Tinder is structured differently

Tinder is the **headline-writing app**. The bio is read in 3–7 seconds. A 500-char paragraph here would actively hurt you — it reads as needy or earnest in a context that rewards punch. The 15–45 word target is from Tinder's own published guidance.

The bio uses the **3-Trait Framework** that high-performing male bios consistently use:
- **Competence signal** — one concrete maturity/stability beat
- **Playful hook** — humor, weirdness, or spark
- **Micro-flirt / invitation** — opens conversation without explicit CTA

# THE BIO

```
Career went: pilot → 3D animation → video games. Songs, pies, and miniature TV sets on the side. Will explain why a Stradivarius is from a volcano if you don't stop me.
```

**~167 chars / ~30 words.** Within Tinder's 15–45 word optimal range.

### How this bio uses the 3-Trait Framework

| Component | The line | What it does |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Competence signal** | *"Career went: pilot → 3D animation → video games."* | Implies range, capability, an unusual but committed work life. The arrow visual is rare on Tinder bios — eye-catching in a 3-second scan. |
| **Playful hook** | *"Songs, pies, and miniature TV sets on the side."* | Three concrete unexpected nouns. "Miniature TV sets" is the line nobody expects — it's the bio's *what?* moment. |
| **Micro-flirt / invitation** | *"Will explain why a Stradivarius is from a volcano if you don't stop me."* | A fact people want to ask about, framed with self-aware over-talker humor. No CTA, but the line dares the reader to engage. |

### Why this works for a 41M serious dater on Tinder

- **No demographic stat-dump.** Age, location, kids — all handled by the structured profile fields above the bio. Spending bio chars on this is wasted.
- **Range signal in one breath.** Pilot + animator + video games + songs + pies + miniature TV. Reads as a guy with a full life — which is the *exact* maturity tell that breaks through to the small slice of serious daters on Tinder.
- **Filter via the closing line.** Women who think the Stradivarius/volcano thing sounds interesting are the right women. Women who don't will keep scrolling. That's the point.
