## Key Stats

- Profiles with bios get **4x more matches** than those without
- **23% of profiles have no bio at all** — filling yours = instant edge over a quarter of all competitors
- Profiles with prompts get **~10% more matches**
- **Optimal bio length: 15–45 words** — short enough to read in the 3–7 seconds women spend on profiles
- Average male right-swipe rate: ~53%; average male match rate: 1–2% (so every advantage matters)

## Algorithm

- **Dynamic Desirability** scoring (post-Elo, since ~2019, refined into 2026)
- Inputs: photo quality (#1 weight), selectivity (swipe right rate matters — too generous = penalized), daily activity, message reply rate
- Verification: +30% match rate uplift
- New-account boost: 24–72 hours of enhanced visibility — your first impressions are extra weighted

## Audience

- Broadest, fastest of any major app
- Decision speed: **3–7 seconds per profile**
- Skews younger than Bumble/Hinge — but still has a ~40+ population, particularly in mid-size cities
- Mostly casual / hookup-leaning, but some serious daters
- For 41M serious daters: Tinder is a *secondary* app — lower hit rate, higher volume

## Unique Features

- Swipe-based matching
- Tinder Explore (niche discovery)
- Top Picks (premium curated daily)
- Super Likes (high-signal interest)
- Tinder Boost / Super Boost (paid visibility)
