# Bumble — Photo Strategy

> Bumble's photo audience is more polished than Hinge's, faster than FB Dating's, and slower than Tinder's.

## What's different on Bumble

- **Women send the first message** → photos must give her confidence she'd actually want to message
- **Slightly more appearance-conscious audience** than Hinge — they want evidence you can dress for a nice dinner
- **24-hour match expiration** → photos need to do enough work that a woman feels confident messaging within a day
- **Verification badge is highly visible and weighted** → photos must look real (no hyper-curated studio energy)

## The 6-photo Bumble framework

| Slot | Purpose | Bumble-specific notes |
|------|---------|------------------------|
| **1** | Strong, clear face photo | Smiling, no sunglasses, no hat. Slight polish OK; avoid full studio. |
| **2** | Dressed-up / nicer-context photo | Bumble women want evidence you can dress up. Turtleneck, button-up, dinner-out wardrobe. |
| **3** | Career / lifestyle signal | One photo that proves you have a real life beyond apps — work setting, studio, on-set, etc. |
| **4** | Dog / warmth signal | Strong differentiation move — most male profiles don't have this; reduces "is he safe?" friction |
| **5** | Social proof — friends, especially women friends | Bumble-specific lever; proof you're trusted in social spaces |
| **6** | Wildcard — travel, activity, candid | Final-slot range signal. Mexico City rooftop, golf, hiking, dressed-up event. |

## What wins on Bumble

- **Genuine smile in slot 1** — Photofeeler T1 data shows measurable advantage over neutral
- **Outdoor / natural lighting** outperforms indoor
- **One clearly dressed-up photo** — Bumble's signature differentiator vs. Tinder
- **Dog photos** — meaningful uplift on Bumble specifically
- **Social photos with women friends** — uniquely high-leverage on Bumble; reduces safety-friction
- **Polish without performance** — well-shot but not "photoshoot energy"

## What loses on Bumble

- **Sunglasses / hats in slot 1** — −12% baseline penalty
- **Mirror selfies** — universal lose; Bumble audience reads them worst
- **Gym mirror with shirt off** — wide-net negative for 30+ Bumble audience
- **Group photos as lead** — slot 1 must be unambiguously you
- **Sexualized photos** — Bumble audience filters these out aggressively
- **Same-shoot stacking** — variety matters
- **All-casual / no-effort wardrobe** — Bumble women want at least one "he can dress up" signal

## Algorithmic levers

- **Verification badge** = **+93% match rate.** Day 1 priority, no exceptions.
- **All 6 photo slots filled** = completeness signal (#1 algorithmic input)
- **Don't iterate photos in the first 30 days** — algorithm needs stable signal to calibrate
- **Set up Photo Verification immediately** — Bumble shows the badge; matches see it; trust spikes
