# Bumble — Research Findings

> Distilled from `APPS/RESEARCH.md` + April 2026 web research on Bumble best-practice.

## Headline Findings

### 1. Verification = +93% match rate
- **Confidence:** High
- **Source:** T2 (Bumble published data)
- **Implication:** This is the **single biggest free win in dating apps, period.** Photo Verification badge → near-doubling of match rate. Nothing else matches it.

### 2. Profile completeness is the #1 algorithmic input
- **Confidence:** High
- **Source:** T2/T3 algorithm analyses
- **Implication:** Fill every field. Use all 6 photos, all 3 prompts, all interest badges. Do not leave anything blank.

### 3. Women message first → bio + prompts must be opener-bait
- **Confidence:** High (platform mechanic)
- **Implication:** Every prompt should leave an obvious "what to say" for the reader. Don't write closed prompts ("My job is great") — write open ones ("I'll fall for you if you have a hobby that goes too far").

### 4. Opening Moves boosted quality conversations 77%
- **Confidence:** Medium-High
- **Source:** T2 Bumble published stat
- **Implication:** Your matches will increasingly use Opening Moves rather than open chat. Your prompts feed their default questions; opinion-heavy prompts give them more material to react to.

### 5. Compliments feature changed the prompt game
- **Source:** Web research April 2026
- **Implication:** In 2026, women can send a Compliment (a message tied to a specific prompt) **before matching.** This means opinion-bait, polarizing, or hot-take prompts are now high-leverage — they invite a Compliment before the match even happens.

### 6. The 2026 voice trend: specificity + light humor over polish
- **Source:** Multiple T3 (RoastDating, RizzBio, VIDA Select)
- **Finding:** "Swipe fatigue" → users reward authenticity and effort over hyper-curated aesthetics. Profiles based on words are making a comeback.
- **Implication:** Don't perform; be specific. Don't curate; show effort.

## What 41M Serious Daters Need to Know About Bumble

- **Bumble is well-aligned with you.** It's the best swipe-app fit for a relationship-oriented 41M. Per `APPS/STRATEGY.md`, Bumble is a **primary** app in your stack.
- **The audience is slightly more polished than Hinge.** Women on Bumble want evidence you can dress for a nicer dinner, that you're stable, that you have an actual life. Photos should reflect this.
- **The "never married, no kids" framing on Bumble:** Inside the bio, integrated into a hook. From STRATEGY.md: *"No kids, never married — I was too busy [funny reason]. Now looking for someone worth slowing down for."* That kind of voice. Or weave it into a prompt answer.
- **Effort is the highest-value signal** — 67% of women prefer effort over income (T2 Hinge Labs data, transferable). A polished profile signals you'll show up.

## What Wins on Bumble

- **Specificity** — "currently training for a half-marathon" beats "I love running"
- **Conversation hooks** — every prompt answer should give the reader an obvious next message
- **Opinion-heavy prompts** — bait Compliments + filter for engaged women
- **Active verbs** — "I'm restoring a 1972 motorcycle" beats "I love bikes"
- **Light humor** — playful, not edgy. Bumble audience reads sarcasm-as-personality less generously than Tinder.
- **Polish without performance** — well-dressed photos, good lighting, clear face — but not "studio shoot" energy
- **Multiple hooks** — give her 3 different things to open with

## What Loses on Bumble

- **Negativity** — "no drama" / "tired of games" / "if you can't handle me at my worst…"
- **Pessimism in prompts** — even self-deprecation reads worse here than on Tinder
- **One-word answers / lazy prompts** — Bumble audience is fast to detect low-effort
- **Generic adjective lists** — "fun, adventurous, loyal"
- **Bragging** — income, car, gym pics
- **Empty prompt slots** — completeness is the #1 algorithmic signal
- **Sexualized photos** — Bumble's audience filters these out aggressively
- **Mirror selfies** — universal lose
- **Sunglasses / hats in slot 1** — −12% likes baseline penalty
