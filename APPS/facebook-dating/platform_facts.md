# Facebook Dating — Platform Mechanics

> Pulled from `APPS/PLATFORMS.md`. Hard limits and structural facts.

## Profile Structure

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Photos + Prompts (combined) | **12 total slots** (at least 1 face photo required) |
| Bio | **500 chars** (sweet spot 3–5 short sentences) |
| Prompts | Included in the 12 content slots |
| Photos | Recommended 6 (lead with strongest face photo) |
| Interests | Pulled from your Facebook profile |

## Available Prompts (examples)

- "The one thing you should know about me..."
- "My ideal weekend..."
- "The quickest way to my heart..."
- (additional prompts available in-app — confirm at profile creation)

## Key Stats

- **21.5M daily users** (T2, Global Dating Insights) — most underrated channel by competition density
- Bio sweet spot: 3–5 short sentences (longer than Tinder; shorter than League's 190 cap allows for substance)
- Prompts should be **1–2 sentences, short and punchy**
- **Completely free** — no premium tier; no paywall on visibility

## Algorithm

- **Predicted engagement scoring** (T2 Meta transparency docs)
- Inputs: predicted engagement level + **shared interests** + **shared groups/events**
- Implication: optimization centers on signal richness in your interests, group memberships, and Instagram integration — not "swipe game" mechanics
- Profile completeness is a **High** weight signal

## Profile Elements

- Separate from main Facebook profile (your normal feed friends don't see it)
- Pulls from your Facebook profile (interests, groups, events)
- Can integrate Instagram photos
- Shared interests / groups / events drive matching surface
- Verification not required (lower lift than Bumble/League)

## Unique Features

- **Secret Crush** — pick up to 9 existing Facebook friends; they only see it if they also picked you on Dating. Uniquely powerful for "people you already know"
- Shared group/event matching surfaces context-rich matches (e.g., both in a Madison hiking group)
- Instagram integration — pull recent posts as additional content
- Largest potential user base of any single platform (Meta network effect)
- No swiping pressure — feels closer to LinkedIn-style profile review

## Why this platform is different

Facebook Dating is the only mainstream app where matching is **community-driven**, not pure profile curation. Shared groups, events, and Facebook friends-of-friends create real-world context before either of you opens a chat. The optimal profile leans into that context — friendly, natural, locally-rooted — rather than the polish-and-curation game that wins on Hinge or The League.
