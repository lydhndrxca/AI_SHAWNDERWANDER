## Hinge

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Photos/Videos | **6 slots** (minimum 4 required) |
| Prompts | **3 displayed** (150 chars each) |
| Bio | No separate bio — prompts ARE the bio |
| Total prompts available | **~105** |

### Prompt Types
- **Text prompts** — standard written answers
- **Photo prompts** — pair a prompt with a photo
- **Video prompts** — record a video answer
- **Voice prompts** — audio response
- **Prompt Polls** — matches choose which answer to respond to

### Top-Performing Prompts (data-backed)
- "I know the best spot in town for..."
- "A life goal of mine..."
- "My simple pleasure..."
- "Two truths and a lie..."
- "My most irrational fear..."
- "The dorkiest thing about me..."
- "Typical Sunday..."
- "Dating me is like..."
- "I want someone who..."
- "A fact about me that surprises people..."
- "My biggest date fail..."
- "Debate this topic with me..."

### Key Stats
- Likes on **text prompts are 47% more likely** to lead to dates than likes on photos
- Filling all 3 prompt slots = **73% more quality matches**
- Recommended strategy: 1 humor + 1 depth + 1 conversation-starter

### Unique Features
- "Designed to be deleted" — relationship-focused
- Likes/comments on specific photos or prompts
- Roses (premium like)
- Standouts feed
- No swiping — intentional profile review

## The League

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Photos | **6 recommended** (minimum 3 for acceptance) |
| Bio ("About Me") | **190 chars** |
| Interests | Pulled from Facebook |
| Education & Profession | Pulled from LinkedIn |

### Bio Formula (recommended 4-part structure)
1. **Hook** — 1 memorable sentence
2. **Credibility** — career/education/passion
3. **Personality** — quirk/humor/values
4. **Call-to-Action** — question inviting response

### Key Stats
- Waitlist-based — profile quality determines acceptance
- LinkedIn and Facebook integration for verification
- Career/education signals matter more here than other apps

### Unique Features
- Waitlist / application process
- LinkedIn verification
- League Tickets (daily curated matches)
- Group events
- Video speed dating
- Exclusivity positioning

## Facebook Dating

| Field | Limit |
|-------|-------|
| Photos + Prompts combined | **12 total** (at least 1 face photo required) |
| Bio | **500 chars** |
| Prompts | Included in the 12 content slots |

### Available Prompts (examples)
- "The one thing you should know about me..."
- "My ideal weekend..."
- "The quickest way to my heart..."

### Key Stats
- Bio sweet spot: 3–5 short sentences
- Prompts should be 1–2 sentences, short and punchy

### Profile Elements
- Separate from main Facebook profile
- Can pull Instagram photos
- Shared interests / groups / events matching

### Unique Features
- Integrated with Facebook ecosystem
- Secret Crush (match with existing friends)
- Shared group/event matching
- Free (no premium tier)
- Instagram integration
- Largest potential user base
